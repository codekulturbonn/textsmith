{% trans %}Diversity and Conduct{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Happily, we are a diverse community who maintain a reputation as a friendly, welcoming and tolerant group.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}We trust users will treat each other in a way that celebrates our diversity and fosters friendliness, collaboration and honesty.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}We believe users have a right to:{% endtrans %}

  • {% trans %}be treated with courtesy, dignity and respect;{% endtrans %}
  • {% trans %}be free from any form of discrimination, victimisation, harassment or bullying;{% endtrans %}
  • {% trans %}enjoy an environment free from unwelcome behaviour, inappropriate language and unsuitable imagery.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Our users come from different countries and cultures, with different views, perspectives, opinions and widely varying backgrounds. Therefore, it is important we all assume everyone is acting in good faith. When inevitable disagreements or conflicts arise, we try as best we can to explain and resolve the problem while giving others the opportunity to reply in kind.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}However, assuming good faith does not prohibit robust discussion or criticism offered in a constructive manner.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}In the face of specific and obvious evidence of malice, be civil and contact us in private for help addressing the situation. Please ensure you substantiate and offer evidence for the basis of your concerns. Such contact will remain confidential, be taken seriously, investigated and dealt with appropriately.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}TextSmith administrators reserve the right to carry out any of the following actions relating to a complaint from a user:{% endtrans %}

  • {% trans %}Persons concerned may be told to stop or modify their behaviour appropriately and a warning will be issued.{% endtrans %}
  • {% trans %}Persons concerned may be warned that enforcement action may be taken if the problem continues.{% endtrans %}
  • {% trans %}Persons concerned may be immediately logged off TextSmith with their account suspended for a clearly defined period of time (including an unlimited permanent suspension).{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}TextSmith logs interactions with the service. We do this to help us better understand how the system is functioning. We also collect other data about users as outlined in our privacy statement.{% endtrans %}
